Executive Summary

Game Concept:
It will be a fighter game in which user plane will be moving in air and will be destroying different objects and enemies fighters that come in its way.
Genre:
  • Action
  • Fighting
  • Shooters
  • Casual
Target Audience:
This game is intended to be developed for children of 3 to 12 years of age.
Project Scope:
It will be a 2D game.
