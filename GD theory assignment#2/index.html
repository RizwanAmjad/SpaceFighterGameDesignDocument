Executive Summary

Game Concept:
It will be a fighter game in which user plane will be moving in space and will be destroying different objects and enemies' fighters that come in its way.
Genre:
  • Action
  • Fighting
  • Shooters
  • Casual
Target Audience:
This game is intended to be developed for children of 3 to 12 years of age.
Project Scope:
It will be a 2D game.

Gameplay

Objectives:
  • The main objectives of this game are:
    • To build a game for childrens.
    • To give the player a thrill of space.
    • To enhance children gaming skills.
  • Objectives of Player for the player for playing your game:
    • Entertainment and Timepass purpose.
    • To reach high levels of game.
    • Children may explore space
Game Progression:
As the player procedes in the game. The player will be:
  • Unlocking different Levels.
  • Unlocking different Weapons and Spaceships.
  • Collecting different items that will help them to get these things during gameplay.
In-Game GUI:
The game UI will consist of:
  • Game Menu to start the game and change different settings.
  • Gameplay mode where the user will interact with the game objects playing the Game.

Mechanics

Rules:
These are the following rules according to which fighter will destroy:
  • When enemy Missile hit the fighter
  • When enemy gun fire hit the fighter
  • When fighter was hit by some astroyed
Combat:
The player moves in space. The space is full of Asteriods and enemies. The Player must destroy the Asteriods and protect himself from emeny attacks.
Physics:
Since this game is in Space so most of the physics is not involved. But still some of the physics is applies to its objects. Such as Different collisions of Bullets and Asteriods or Bullets and Spaceships or Enemies.
